## Detailed description

UnShrimp is a playful posture companion for anyone who spends long hours at a desk.

**What it does**

- Opens a monitor page in Chrome and uses your webcam to track seated upper-body posture
- Calibrates to *your* normal sitting position in about five seconds
- Shows a live Shrimp Index, posture status, and friendly feedback
- Plays an optional alert sound after sustained slouching

**Privacy first**

- All processing happens **on your device**
- No photos or video are uploaded or stored
- Calibration is saved locally in your browser only

**How to use**

1. Click the UnShrimp icon → **Open Monitor**
2. Click **Begin Unshrimping** and allow camera access
3. Sit normally, then **Re-measure My Shrimpness** (calibrate)
4. Keep the monitor tab open while you work

**Note:** This is a wellness and ergonomics helper, not medical advice. For serious pain or injury, consult a professional.
